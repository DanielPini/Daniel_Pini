Web Engineering & Delivery

Delivery for me means keeping scope honest, making iteration cheap, and shipping pieces that can breathe. I lean on simple modules, clear naming, and small performance guardrails rather than heavy abstraction.

When something slows designers or artists, I shape a focused tool instead of adding a framework. When performance dips, I profile, trim, and move on—no theatrics.
